Our mid-term goal

To set up beneficiation and smelting plants to produce refined pure metals; so as to attract premium pricing for our refined products and facilitate listing and trading opportunities on the international metal exchanges.

Our long-term goal

To become a leading, sustainable and efficient mining company. We will achieve this by creating value for our shareholders by minimizing risks related to exploration, mining, and processing of our mineral resources and increasing efficiency. Our primary focus is on Lithium and Gold production in Nigeria and other African countries.
